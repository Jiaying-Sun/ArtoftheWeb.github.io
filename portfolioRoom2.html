My Age Clock

The Age Clock is inspired by tree rings, with the green color representing leaves and the brown color representing the tree. The three circles indicate the hour hand, minute hand, and second hand, while the tree rings themselves represent the passing of time. Every time my age increases by one year, the clock adds another tree ring to symbolize the passing year, and the constantly increasing number on the clock represents my age.
